Our Specialities Products

Our business prides on serving you spices, pulses, lentils, grains and canned products of the very best quality.

Lentils

Lentils are small, nutritious legumes that are widely used in cooking for their high protein and fiber content.

Kabuli Chickpeas

Kabuli chickpeas are healthy legumes, high in protein and fiber, with a nutty flavor. Versatile in cooking.

Mung Beans

Mung beans are small legumes with nutty flavor, high in protein and fiber. Used in soups, curries, salads.

Desi Chickpeas

Desi chickpeas are legumes with a strong nutty flavor and firm texture, commonly used in curries, stews, salads, and snacks.

Yellow Split Peas

Yellow split peas are legumes with a mild flavor and soft texture, often used in soups, stews, and curries for their high protein and fiber content.

Wheat Grain

Wheat grain is a nutritious cereal with a mild, nutty flavor, high in fiber, protein, and various nutrients, commonly used in baked goods and cereals.

Red Sorghum

Red sorghum is a gluten-free cereal with a mild, nutty flavor, rich in antioxidants, fiber, and protein, commonly used in porridges, salads, and baked goods.

Faba Beans

Faba beans are protein-rich legumes with a buttery texture and nutty flavor, used in stews, soups, salads, and dips.

Kaspa Peas

Kaspa peas are small, sweet peas with a smooth texture and nutty flavor, commonly used in soups, stews, salads, and casseroles.
